Sound settings

Below you will see all options relating to the sound your device will play at activation.

  • Make chosen sound default

  • Mute device

Here you can find all available sounds and mark one off to choose as a default. To preview a sound for 15 seconds, click it and it will be selected. If you now select 'Make chosen sound default' the respective audio will be sent to the neck pillow.
